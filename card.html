Some History...

Originally from Orient rats expanded and invaded the whole world as soon as human beings started to travel the world: boats, planes,...Where there are humans, there are rats. Rats surround humans environment for our sedentarity tends to give them everything they need to live and expand durably: home and food. Rats have known how to adapt to each era.


And because of the proximity with human beings, and unhealthiness of cities during the middle age, rats were set responsible for bringing plague to humans, and were feared and depicted as noxious. Still nowadays, rats are still associated to the image of diseases and nuisance.


Rats belong to the type of rattus, and count more than fifty species. It's been about 180 years that brown rats, have become pets for human being, for they are more of a stay-at-home kind than the other species. Today, even though rats are massively used for experimetation, they have really become animals that humans are taking care of, such as dogs and cats.

Description

  • Name : Rattus norvegicus domestica, known as fancy rats.
  • Lifespan : not more than three years.
  • Habitat : fancy rats are sociable and lived in cage with all the comfort they need.
  • Characteristics :
    • length : 20 to 28 centimeters long, with a tail of 13 to 15 cm.
    • weigth : up to 700g.
    • life style : crepuscular.
    • diet : omnivore.

Interesting facts

Fancy rats is a very lovely pet, simple to raise. They are very curious and really smart; they can adapt easily to their environment. These adorable animals love to be wheedled, they are loyal and easily trained, even though they are quite independent. All these characteristics make them comparable to both cats and dogs.
